ABOUT

Theme: Arresters for FAIL SAFE protection

Surge Arrester - The most critical equipment for safe & reliable operation of Electrical Power System play an important role in limiting Transient over voltages, Switching &lightning surges on Main Equipment like Transformers, Reactors & Circuit Breakers etc. The Indian Surge Arrester Industry has designed, tested and successfully commissioned Polymer housed Surge Arresters upto 1200kV at National Test station, Bina(India).

PAST CONFERENCE

SURGEPROTECH will give an opportunity to participants from various stakeholder groups in the Surge Arrester value chain to share their expertise. The tutorial cum conference will also provide detailed insights on various key system components and their latest technological trends, keeping in mind an end-to-end solutions scenario in the expected smart world. This tutorial cum conference is envisaged to benefit utilities, industries, various government bodies and other stake-holders in strengthening their technological choices, offering them the option to empower and utilize existing infrastructure for a sustainable future.
