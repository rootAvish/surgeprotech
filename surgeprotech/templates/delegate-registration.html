Surgeprotech 2016

     

SURGEPROTECH will give an opportunity to participants from various stakeholder groups in the Surge Arrester value chain to share their expertise. The tutorial cum conference will also provide detailed insights on various key system components and their latest technological trends, keeping in mind an end-to-end solutions scenario in the expected smart world. The challenges being faced by different stake holders will be discussed at length to enrich the experience of all the participants, and enchance their knowledge with a real-world approach. This tutorial cum conference is envisaged to benefit utilities, industries, various government bodies and other stake-holders in strengthening their technological choices, while offering them the option to empower and utilize existing infrastructure for a sustainable future.
